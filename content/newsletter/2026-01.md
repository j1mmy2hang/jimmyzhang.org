### Personal Statement Reviewer

This is my first attempt at turning a simple AI prompt into a web app—just prompt + AI at its core, with some design and UX. 

To my surprise, I received genuine reviews from people saying it actually helped with their essays.

I think this project is also especially significant for me because it marks a transformation that in my think-trend. A year ago I would have dismissed the project as a gimmick. But now actually hearing good feedback from people around me, I realized that instead of designing grand systems, start connecting with and help people around me. 

try it out -- https://ps-review.jimmyzhang.org
