---

The start of the month is marked by the Last School Day of my high school. I have always quite rejected the notion celebrating this day, so I quickly packed up and went directly back home in China for a three week break before I come back for my IB exam. 

After I'm back, I quickly published [Delphi](https://delphi-web.netlify.app), an AI chatbot better at listening, asking questions, and helping you think clearly. For myself, it generates quite a surprising level of emotional intelligence and depth during the first few times using. 

Staying home has been great for deep focus with my monitor setup. I also rediscovered the analog notebook as a powerful tool for thinking — [[computer-as-tool]]. 

I started actually thinking about the academic pathway that I want to pursue at University of Michigan, and landed on the intersections of fields like Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning, Game Theory, and mechanism design. 

I spent the bulk of the month re-designing https://jimmyzhang.org with Claude Code. In fact, a $20/month subscription successfully enabled to get rid of my subscription for Obsidian Publish and Squarespace. Read more about my reflection here — [[ai-eats-saas]]. I also reflected on the success of AI as a mass-consumer product here — [[ai-success]]。

Finally, I finished reading half of the novel [一句顶一万句 (Someone to Talk To)](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/35398341-someone-to-talk-to) by Zhenyun Liu (刘震云). I have long neglected the reading of Chinese literary work, and I feel great to be back in the game. I am especially astonished by the complexity of human relationships and life itself that the novel portrays, so many detailed captures of the authentic complexities of human life.  

Before I fly back to the Netherlands, a small story makes me feel the beauty and power of art — [[flower-by-lake]]。

After I came back, I took my IB exam for Physics. Reviewing physics made me think about [[ib-physics]]. Meanwhile, I published a small app that I made for myself to learn french called [Cahier](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C-CrTilSquI). I also quickly read the book [[Finite and Infinite Games]], and acquired some deeper understanding regarding how I would view life as game and myself as a player. 

With that concludes April 2026.
