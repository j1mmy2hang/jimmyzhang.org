## Qchat

![[Screenshot 20260324 at 181419@2x.jpg]]

<u>A lightweight and minimalistic LLM chat client. </u>

No conversation history, no memory, no deep research. The idea is just a clean space for chatting with AI, as a replacement to the poorly designed ChatGPT desktop app or webpage. 

**An innovative design** -- hitting "tab" to switch between LLM model, without need for mouse interaction.
