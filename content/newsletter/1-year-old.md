Dear friend, 

In April 2025, I published jimmyzhang.space, my knowledge garden containing thousands of notes of mine. Not long later, I acquired the domain jimmyzhang.org as well and set up my personal website on it using squarespace.

Since then, I have been shipping monthly updates on jimmyzhang.space, sharing books I read, notes I took, essays I wrote, and projects I made. jimmyzhang.org remained where I share my photos, writings, and other misc. Meanwhile, I also set up roadmap.jimmyzhang.org as my public roadmap. 

One year later, I decided to merge the two domains and everything about me into a fully re-designed https://jimmyzhang.org, turning it into the only official domain of Jimmy Zhang. 

jimmyzhang.org now contains everything about me — all my notes (over 3000 and counting), writings, projects, photos, and more. 

So, my friends, at its one-year anniversary, proudly I (re-)introduce you to https://jimmyzhang.org 

![[1-year-of-jimmyzhang-org.webp]]

It is more than a personal website. More importantly, it is an effort to distill myself into a file system, putting all of my life's work in a folder. The backend of the entire site is a folder of markdown notes, that is really what it is. It is also optimized for agent navigation, so any AI agent (OpenClaw, Codex, Claude Code, etc.) can freely access my folder at https://github.com/j1mmy2hang/jimmyzhang.org — to read and retrieve however they want. 

This folder is, in fact, all of my life's work, and all that I hope to leave to this world if I were to die any day from now on. 

My entire life's work presented to you. 

Sign up for the monthly update below. 

Sincerely,
Jimmy Zhang
