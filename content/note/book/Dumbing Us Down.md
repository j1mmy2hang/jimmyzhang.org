## My Atomic Notes

### The Reality

[[True education is a priviledge]]
[[Mass schooling damages children]]

[[The hidden curriculum that school really teaches]]
--> the true aim --  [[Compulsory-government-monopoly-mass-schooling serves as a social engineering project to produce manageable, dependent, and incomplete individuals unfit for self-governance or a rich inner life, for factories and corporations]]

[[Schools break families and communities and disconnect students from the world]]

[[Schooling and education are mutually exclusive]]

### The Ideal

[[Education is the art of sculpture not painting]]

[[A good education fosters self-knowledge through real-world experiences in communities, independent study, and solitude]]

[[Education is fundamentally about cultivating unique and free souls]]

- [[Agency is earned]]
- [[We don't need school reform, but to create more opportunities and choices outside of school]]
- [[Dismantle the monopoly, embrace market principles, and trust families and local communities to define and their their own educational paths]]
