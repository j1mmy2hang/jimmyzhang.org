## Implications

[[The creation of public knowledge is a high-stakes act that removes the exit strategy]]

**Individual, relationship level**: [[Humans tend to strategically avoid common knowledge and replace with euphemisms and innuendo to navigate relationships]]

[[Radical honesty is anti-social]]

**Society level**: [[Societies suppress truth to maintain shared fiction with rational hypocrisy]]

[[虚伪是文明的润滑剂]]
[[Sometimes peace can only exist in grey area]]

but at the end of the day [[Human society needs both truth and hypocrisy to function]]
