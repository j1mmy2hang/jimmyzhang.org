# Afterword

Don't tell me you had a wonderful meeting with me. Tell me what you are going to do on Monday that's different. 

Peter Drucker did not create the discipline of management because it was a smart idea. He created it because he had the courage to ask what he could do to strengthen the institutions of society -- and the society itself -- against the horrors of the 20th century.
