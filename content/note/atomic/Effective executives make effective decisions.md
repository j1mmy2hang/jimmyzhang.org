[[Effective decisions should be strategic & made at the highest conceptual level of understanding]] 
 
## The Decision-Making Process / Elements

1. [[Treat the problem as generic and solve it through a decision which established a rule or principle]]
2. [[Effective decisions define the boundary conditions clearly|Define the boundary conditions]]
3.  [[What is right over what is acceptable]]
4. Convert the decision into action -- [[The action to carry it out must be built into the decision itself]]
5.  [[Feedback is essential to check reality against expectation of a decision]]

## Points of notices

- [[The right decision demands adequate disagreement]]
- [[Weigh the cost and gain of action against the risk of inaction before decision-making]]
- [[Courage & opportunity over judgement]]
