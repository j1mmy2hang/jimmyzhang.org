The post hoc fallacy: *post hoc, ergo propter hoc* -- after this, therefore because of this
in politics: people take credit and blame others for detrimental changes that they do not cause

> When two things are both very striking, and one occurs right after the other, then the first is especially likely to be considered the cause of the second.
