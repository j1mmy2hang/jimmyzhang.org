north:: [[From scarcity to abundance]]

To love is to let go. — You don’t truly love something if your love depends on *possessing* it.

Love should only depends on *using* and *enjoying* it at this very moment -- everything is flow, you can't hold on to something forever

Know that you might lose her any day at any moment, so that you can better enjoy the moment right now. 

Just like acknowledging death helps us live a better life
