In the 20th Century, we had the "6 O'clock News." It wasn't always true, but it was **Common Knowledge**. We all knew what the main stories were.

In 2025, Algorithms give everyone a personalized reality (Infinite Private Knowledge).

**The Crisis:** Without a central "Public Square" to generate Common Knowledge, we cannot coordinate on big issues (Climate Change, Pandemic response, Governance). We are stuck in 10,000 separate "Private Knowledge" bubbles.

[[Human society needs both truth and hypocrisy to function]]
