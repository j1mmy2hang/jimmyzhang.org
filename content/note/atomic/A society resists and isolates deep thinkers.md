**Society prioritizing comformity over disruptive truth** -- will to live vs will to truth

2. **Resulting architecture:** Conformity‑bias neural wiring + pain of ostracism.
3. **Implementation:** Groupthink, antisocial punishment, identity signaling, institutional metrics.
4. **Modern amplifiers:** Attention algorithms, bureaucratic risk aversion, populist politics.
5. **Outcome:** Deep thinkers face systematic resistance and social isolation _unless_ special counter‑structures intervene.
