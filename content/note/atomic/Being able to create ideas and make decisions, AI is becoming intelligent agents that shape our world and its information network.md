## New Information Networks

[[Computers serve as links & members in information networks]]

First, computer-to-human chains, in which computers mediate between humans and occasionally control humans.

Second, computer-to-computer chains are emerging in which computers interact with one another on their own. Humans are excluded from these loops and have difficulty even understanding what’s happening inside them.
