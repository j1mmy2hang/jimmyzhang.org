In a world of infinite choice, providing more options is not a feature; it's a bug. The greatest value you can offer customers is to save them time by filtering the noise and presenting them with only what is relevant and valuable to them.

[[The moat of software in the age of AI is an opinionated perspective on the solution]]

## Helping audience define their identity

By providing filters, you are helping people discover and define who they are. A great recommendation system doesn't just sell a product; it reveals something to the user about their own tastes.

> "We can use the mass filtering that is coming to sharpen who we are, for the personalization of our own person... We don’t know ourselves very well. To some degree we will rely on the filters to tell us what we want. Not as slave masters, but as a mirror."

**Lesson:** Your brand can become a partner in your customer's journey of self-discovery. The more your filters help them understand themselves, the more indispensable your service will become.
