Language is (only) a human construct. 

We come into the world without words, with all the other wonderful sensory inputs. 

Gradually, language gains ascendency.

> We assume that the dominance of language forms not only the foundation of how we communicate, but also the foundation of how we think—and in fact for centuries, we have been taught to believe just that.
