## school ≠ education

学校试图垄断执行教育的权力和资源 来制造 only we are qualified to educate you 的假相

<u>but the truth is:</u>
education can take place everywhere, and most of them better than schools

**schools are not necessarily where education takes place**
**better education mostly takes place outside school**

always find the best way to educate yourself -- 目标
school is just one of the means -- 手段
