China & American are two giant powers with mutually incompatible ambitions

> Sino-American relations have collapsed, raising the prospect of a globe divided into rival camps, even as other countries insist that they have no desire to pick sides. Wary but profitable coexistence has turned into a contest for primacy in the 21st century.

The confrontation is alarming because logic guides each side.
