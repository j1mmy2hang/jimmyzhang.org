## 记笔记不是为了记住，而是为了忘记

"cognitive offloading"

笔记软件、书签工具，本质上是给大脑买的保险。它们让我们相信："东西存在那里，随时可以找回来。"有了这种安全感，我们才能放心地忘记。

让我们**既能忘记，又不真的失去**。

> Note and bookmarks apps need to make us feel safe. Safe that we can save everything and forget it, that it’ll be there when we come back. Anything could be that safe place, even a plain document, a scratchpad, that gets longer the more things you add to it.
