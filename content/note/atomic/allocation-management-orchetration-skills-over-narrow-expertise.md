> We’re moving rapidly to an allocation economy, where individuals won’t be judged by the limits of subject matter expertise, but instead on how well they can allocate and manage AI resources to get work done. The key skills needed to get the most out of AI are the same skills good managers possess—goal setting, clear communication, effective feedback, and constant learning.
