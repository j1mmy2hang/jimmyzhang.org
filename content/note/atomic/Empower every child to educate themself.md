In the AI age, we don't need no teachers, curriculums, or schools. 
All we need is the agency to reclaim our own education from institutions, regain the ability of self-directed learning, rediscover who we are, and reassert who we want to be in this world. 

I help young people learn by guiding and empowering them to define their own life, design their own path, and develop their own system of learning to achieve their dream.

On one hand, learning is about the collection and management of information & knowledge -- it's about locating what's valuable and finding what matters in a world full of noise. It's about dveloping wisdom that can be applied to different fields of life. 

On the other hand, learning is much more than knowledge -- it's about turning knowledge into the force that help us go to where we want to and achieve our goals. 

The age of learning for certificate is over. The future only belongs to the meta-learners who have clear goals and learning abilities that prepare them for whatever is to come.
