respect =
1. treating someone like a person
2. treating someone like an authority

> And sometimes people who are used to being treated like an authority say "if you won't respect me I won't respect you" and they mean "if you won't treat me like an authority I won't treat you like a person". They think they're being fair but they aren't, and it's not OK.
