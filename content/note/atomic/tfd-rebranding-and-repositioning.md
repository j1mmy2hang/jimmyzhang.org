## 1. Key Observations

### We don't have a clear sense of purpose

We lack a working missions statement / value proposition.

> What do we want to achieve with this project?
> What effect do we hope to achieve?
> Why do we want to achieve those goals?

### We fail to track & measure our impact

> Do people really enjoy our issue?
> Do they really listen to the podcast?
> Are we making a positive impact in the community?

### We fail to engage the community

Currently it felt more like self-entertainment

> Are people execited for every new issue to come out?
> Do they feel incentivized & excited to submit their pieces?
> Do they feel safe to share their true thoughts?

### The form of physical newsaper is costly, unsustianable, and outdated

Newspapers was invented in the 16th Century.

## 2. Proposed Solution

> [!Solution]
> Transitioning form **physical newspaper** to **online community platform**

Potential platform: Linkedin group, Circle

### Benefits

- more engaging (fostering communitation)
- easier to scale
- more opportunities & flexibility
	- multi-media -- maybe collab with MIN (both as school media)
	- multi-fucntion -- e.g. borrowing food, requesting for door opening, mensa snack check, buying & selling, etc.)

*We could still compile the best posts into physical monthly issue for print*

## 3. Development Vision

UWCM newspaper --> UWCM community platform (text-based)
--> UWCM multi-media & multi-function community platform

--> UWC multi-media & multi-function community platform


> [!Vision]
> Final Product = a student-led global, cross-campus, multi-function UWC online community
