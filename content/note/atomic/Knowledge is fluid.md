- **Context-dependent** — Knowledge shifts depending on circumstances. What is “true” or “useful” in one situation may not be in another.

- **Evolving** — New discoveries, new perspectives, or better models constantly reshape what we know.

- **Distributed** — Knowledge doesn’t live in one mind but across people, systems, cultures, and technologies.
    
- **Interpretive** — Different individuals and communities interpret and apply knowledge differently

- **Interconnected** — Pieces of knowledge are not isolated; they influence and modify each other when combined.
