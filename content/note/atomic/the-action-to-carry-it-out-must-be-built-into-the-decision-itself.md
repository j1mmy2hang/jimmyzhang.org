---
title: "The action to carry it out must be built into the decision itself"

created: 2024-10-21
tags:
  - action
  - decision-making
---
A decision will not become effective unless the action commitments have been built into the decision from the start. 

No decision has been made unless carrying out specific steps has become someone's work assignment and responsibility. Until then, there are only good intentions. 

Unless a decision has degenerated into work, it is not a decision; it is at best a good intention.
