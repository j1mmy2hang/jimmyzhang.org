在有限游戏里玩无限游戏
用无限游戏的姿态认真参与有限游戏

> "Finite games can be played within an infinite game, but an infinite game cannot be played within a finite game... Infinite players enter into finite games with all the appropriate energy and self-veiling, but they do so without the seriousness of finite players."

无限玩家依然会去扮演社会角色，但他们**充满游戏精神（Playful）**。他们清楚地知道自己戴着面具，并且向别人承认这一点。他们不是为了拿到那个Title去迎合规则，他们只是借用这个有限游戏的场域，去和真实的“人”（而不是角色）产生深刻的连接 — [[Finite game as bridge to touch people]]

不要抱怨 finite game 而消沉 — [[你现在就是自由的]]

Even if you are playing a finite game, you are playing it within an infinite game as long as you have an infinite game mindset — [[Look for experience, people, and surprises, not winning]]
