It is amazing how many things they find that can be done and are worthwhile doing. While the others complain about their inability to do anything, the effective executives go ahead and do.

The assertion that somebody else will not let me do anything should always be suspected as a cover up for inertia. Even where the situation does set limitations and everyone lives and works within rather stringent limitations, there are usually important, meaningful, and pertinent things that can be done.
