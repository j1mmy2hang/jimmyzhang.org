# Life's Essential Consumption: Calories and Tokens

## The Dual Engine Theory

Human existence can be distilled to two fundamental consumption patterns:

**Calories** - Fuel for biological processes
- Physical movement and survival
- Brain function and cellular repair
- Growth and reproduction

**Tokens** - Fuel for cognitive processes
- Information processing and decision-making
- Communication and relationship building
- Learning and creative output

## The Efficiency Paradox

Modern life presents an interesting optimization challenge:
- We've minimized calorie expenditure (sedentary lifestyles)
- While maximizing token consumption (information overload)

This creates an imbalance - our bodies evolved for high calorie/low token environments, but we now live in low calorie/high token conditions.
