加
你知道你这一辈子
恐怕都再不会相见的人
的微信
只为说一句再见
还有什么更浪漫的事

这纯属自我感动
要笑吐了

adding wechat with people 
even though they are not your friend
and you know for the rest of your life
you will never meet again

just to say hi
and say goodbye
what’s more romantic than that
