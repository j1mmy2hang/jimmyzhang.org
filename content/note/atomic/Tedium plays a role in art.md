what is the role of tedium in the work that you are doing?
what happens when it takes no effort, no craftsmanship to take an idea to its fruition, to its first execution?
What is lost when an idea takes no craftsmanship?

> There is something to the fact that in that process of the tedium, sometimes that is what creates the flow of art and that leads to a more creative discovery.

Great art has a spirit of its own that is expressed through the medium that it is carried in.
