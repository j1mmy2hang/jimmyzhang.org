深度无聊是精神放松的终极状态

e.g. 行走时感到无聊
无法忍受无聊 -- 焦虑、烦躁，寻找其他活动和刺激 r
对无聊更有耐心 -- 发明新的行走方式：舞蹈
同线性比直的走路相比较，动作花哨舞蹈显得过于铺张，完全不符合效绩原则的要求

> Our continued quest for efficiency discouraged us from looking too deeply. The pressure to deliver doesn't grant us time to consider all possibilities.

> The artist actively works to experience life slowly and to re-experience the same thing anew. To read slowly and to read and read again.
