Courage rather than analysis dictates the true important rules for identifying priorities. 

Achievement depends less on ability in doing the actual work than on the courage to go after opportunity. 

A decision requires courage as much as it requires judgment.

找对了机会和方向，其他只要努力干活大差不差就行。

- Pick the [[Future over Past|future against the past ]]
- Focus on opportunity rather than on the problem
- [[教育不是让孩子扮演他人，而是让孩子成为自己|Choose your own direction]] rather than climb on the bandwagon
- Aim high, aim for something that will make a difference rather than something that is "safe" and easy to do 

> Without courage, an executive in possession of the most brilliant idea in history can only ponder what might be. With courage, knowledge becomes productive.
