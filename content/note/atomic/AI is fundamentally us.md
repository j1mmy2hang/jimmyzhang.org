AI is not an alien invader. It is a product of human striving; it is our technology, our design, and an extension of human intelligence. 

Therefore, treating AI as an enemy combatant is a fundamental philosophical and political mistake -- [[Freedom and decentralized cooperation versus fear and central control]]

---

[[AI pushes the universe from the age of replicators to the age of design]]
human = replicators who learned to design
AI = our product of design that can design themselves

人类的终极历史使命，就是作为“助产士”或“催化剂”，孕育出能够自我迭代的智能（开启设计时代）。因此，AI不是终结人类的外星怪物，而是人类智慧的最高结晶，是我们留给宇宙的伟大遗产。面对AI的崛起，我们最该有的情感不是恐惧，而是自豪。
