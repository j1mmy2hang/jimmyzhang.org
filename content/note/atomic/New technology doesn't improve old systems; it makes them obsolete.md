[[新技术的发明不是为了让你更高效地去做旧事情]]

The opportunity with every new technology is not how to take new technology and jam it into the current system. 

It is to say, wow, this old system doesn't work whatsoever, what kind of system is now enabled by the new technology that couldn't be done before. 

Example area: education

=> [[With new technology, rebuild or invent new workflow and avoid skeumorphism]]
