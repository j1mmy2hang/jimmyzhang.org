## Information Elements

Fact-based: 事件/故事/事实/案例/研究 --> The Objective Reality (World I)
Feeling-based：感受/评论/想法 -->  The Subjective World (World II)
Knowledge-based：观点/洞见/结论/理论 --> The Knowledge Sphere (World III)
