Rather than being acknowledged as a use of power.

反感把<u>权力</u>包装成<u>为你好</u>

I hate it when people present their control as if it’s based on facts or morality, instead of openly admitting they are enforcing a rule or decision due to their power.

e.g. "You don't need to use your laptop."
Bro, you don't get to decide what I need and do not need!

You can say that I'm *not allowed to* use my laptop.
I'm OK with that because you have power over me and i admit it

But I hate people pretending to stand on the side of fact and pointing out wha t I need and do not need when my need can only determined by me and not anybody else.

You are exerting a power, not stating a moral. You dont have rights on your side.
