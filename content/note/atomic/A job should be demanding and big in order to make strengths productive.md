Only if the job is big and demanding to begin with will it enable a man to rise to new demands of a changed situation. 

Whatever his strength, he should have a chance to find full play. 

It is equally important for him to find out as early as possible whether he is indeed in the right place or even in the right kind of work. 
"Am I in the right work and in the right place for my strengths to shine? "

The ones who are enthusiastic and who in turn have the results to show for their work are the ones whose abilities are being challenged and used 

The young knowledge worker whose job is too small to challenge and test his abilities either leaves or declines rapidly into premature middle age, soured, cynical, and unproductive.
