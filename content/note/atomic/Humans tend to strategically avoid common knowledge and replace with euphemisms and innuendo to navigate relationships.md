[[The creation of public knowledge is a high-stakes act that removes the exit strategy]]

Therefore, we strategically **avoid** Common Knowledge (using politeness, euphemisms, and innuendo) to keep our options open and to protect our relationships from shattering when we try to negotiate the terms.

east:: [[Calling out and being a "whistleblower" breaks relationship dynamic by creating new public knowledge]]

---
### Social relationships = coordination games

1. **Communal Sharing:** "What's mine is yours." (Family, lovers).
2. **Authority Ranking:** "You command, I obey." (Boss/employee, Officer/Civilian).
3. **Equality Matching:** "Tit for tat." (Exchanging favors, splitting the bill).
4. **Market Pricing:** "I pay, you deliver." (Buying a car).

## Romantic Relationship

Before confirming a relationship: avoid, euphemism, can exit easily gracefully (I never wanted you)

After confirming a relationship: [[It is important in a romantic relationship to continuously confirm public knowledge]]
