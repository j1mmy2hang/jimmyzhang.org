> It's good to be idealistic, but be prepared to be misunderstood. Anyone working on a big vision will get caught crazy, even if you end up right. Anyone working on a complex problem will get blamed for not fully understanding the challenge, even though it's impossible to know everything upfront. Anyone taking initiative will get criticized for moving too fast, because there's always someone who wants to slow you down.

[[There is great power in not knowing]]
