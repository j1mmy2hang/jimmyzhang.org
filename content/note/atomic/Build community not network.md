A **Community** is a group of people bound by shared physical space, mutual trust, and interdependent relationships (a neighborhood, a school, a congregation). 

A **Network** is a collection of individual nodes connected by shared interests or data points, often on a digital platform (your LinkedIn connections, a Facebook group). Networks connect; communities bind.

数字化的“网络”虽然可以提供广泛的联系，但往往是浅薄和功利性的。而“社区”则是在模拟世界（analog world）中通过面对面互动建立的，它包含了深厚的情感联系、信任和归属感，这对个人的幸福感和社会的健康发展至关重要。
