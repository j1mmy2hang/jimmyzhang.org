### Social relationships = coordination games

1. **Communal Sharing:** "What's mine is yours." (Family, lovers).
2. **Authority Ranking:** "You command, I obey." (Boss/employee, Officer/Civilian).
3. **Equality Matching:** "Tit for tat." (Exchanging favors, splitting the bill).
4. **Market Pricing:** "I pay, you deliver." (Buying a car).
