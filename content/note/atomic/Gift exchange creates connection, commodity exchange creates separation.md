## Commodity vs Gift Exchange

> "It is the cardinal difference between gift and commodity exchange that a gift establishes a feeling-bond between two people, while the sale of a commodity leaves no necessary connection."

> "It is this element of relationship which leads me to speak of gift exchange as an **'erotic'** commerce, opposing **eros** (the principle of attraction, union, involvement which binds together) to **logos** (reason and logic in general, the principle of differentiation in particular). A market economy is an emanation of logos."

人类的交换分为两种

Commodity Exchange -- Logos
理性、边界、分离
商品交换一旦完成，人与人之间的关系就结束了。

Gift Exchange -- Eros
attraction, union, involvement which binds together
