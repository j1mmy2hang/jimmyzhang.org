---
title: "School delays the moment for us to define ourselves"

created: 2026-02-04 16:24
---
School is just a socially acceptable way to pause time. 

We cling to the structure of education because it gives us a label ("student") that protects us from the scary question: "Who are you when no one is telling you what to do?" It is about delaying the moment you have to define yourself.
