#### 1. Impartation of Knowledge

[[学校的主要作用不再是帮助我们获得信息和知识]]

[[人类的进步 = 学习 + 创新]]
[[学习的目的是知识的传递以达到物种文明的进化]]

#### 2. Cultivation of Learning Ability

[[Learning is one of the most essential skills for the 21st Century]]
[[学历的作用是用来证明我们有学习的能力]]
[[Students are forced to learn but without being taught how to learn]]
[[The purpose of school is to teach students how to learn]]
[[The goal of education is de-schooling]]

[[A good education seeks to find the right learning method for a particular brain]]

#### 3. Liberation of Potential

[[Liberation = enlightenment + empowerment]]

[[Industrial model of education is standardization & reducing to tools]]
