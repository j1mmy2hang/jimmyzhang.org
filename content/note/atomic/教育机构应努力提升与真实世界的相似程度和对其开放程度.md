学校 / 教育机构的评价标准 -- 和真实社会，真实世界的相似程度和开放程度。

是否让学生感受体验真实的世界？是否让学生自由的在模拟环境中探索，同时能够直接接触到真实世界而不成为一个 bubble？

- **Resemblance** — Does the internal social environment mirror the complexity of the real world? Are students exposed to genuine dynamics of power, collaboration, competition, trust, and betrayal — not sanitized simulations?
- **Openness** — Does the institution allow students to reach _outward_ into the real world, rather than becoming a self-contained bubble?
