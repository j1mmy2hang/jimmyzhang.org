回收能够重新赋予废物独特的价值

在Recycling Garden中，每一个瓶子（即使是作为装饰品挂在天空中），每一个废料，都闪耀着它们独特的价值的光辉。

In the Recycling Garden, every bottle—even those suspended as ornaments in the sky—and every piece of discarded material radiates the brilliance of its unique value.
