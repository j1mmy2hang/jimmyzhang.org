---
title: "Repetitive practice works because of mentality not repetition"

tags:
  - repetition
  - mentality
  - improvement
---
During practice, you remember your successes, your good qualities and characteristics, and forget your failures.

> If mere repetition were the key to improved skill, his practice should make him more expert at missing the ball than hitting it. However, even though the misses outnumber the hits, he hits the ball more successfully because **his mind remembers, reinforces, and dwells on the successful attempts rather than the misses**.
