>  The man who focuses on efforts and who stretches his downward authority is a subordinate no matter how exalted his title and rank. But the man who focuses on contribution and who takes responsibility for results - no matter how junior - is in the most literal sense of the phrase "top management". He holds himself accountable for the performance of the whole.
