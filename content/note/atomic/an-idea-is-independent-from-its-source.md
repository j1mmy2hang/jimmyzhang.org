A source only <u>refers to / contributes to / uses</u> that idea

an idea = an independent entity that 有自己的独立价值
an idea doesn't rely on a particular source to exist

> Free knowledge from its sources

A source cannot claim any idea to be its own ([[nothing-created-is-original]])
knowledge is meant to be shared, open-source, and recreated

当然可以 acknowledge 不同的 source 和 contributor (just like Github)

但很多 idea 最初的 source 已经不可考也没有意义考证
那就让我们承认它的独立存在，给它一个命名
