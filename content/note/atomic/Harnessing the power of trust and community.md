## Simple vs Complex Contagion

simple contagion = low risk
complex contagion = require individuals to make a substantial personal investment due to the costs or risks involved, including reputational or social risks, personal risks, and personal effort

--> a single recommendation is rarely enough

We embrace complex contagions based on norms, not knowledge.
