Good decision-making comes down to two things:
1. Knowing how to get what you want 
2. **Knowing what's worth wanting**

[[Effective decisions are not necessarily good decisions|Effective ≠ Good Decisions]]

*phronesis* — the wisdom of knowing how to order your life to achieve the best results (knowing what's good and how to achieve them)

importance 
-- The quality of what you pursue determines the quality of your life.
