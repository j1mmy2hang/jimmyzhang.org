Most people do not know how to deal with loneliness.

They seek to escape solitude by scrolling on social media, reaching for the bottle, or chasing fleeting intimacy. 

Knowing how to entertain yourself is an important skill and important lesson of life. 

Being able to spend time with yourself, away from the any external simulation, is where deep thinking, self-exploration, and creativity happen. Solitude can be transformed into a powerful drive for personal achievement and a sense of fullfilment. 

At the end of the day, life is a lonely journey. And if we never learned to deal with that loneliness, we might never understand our life truly.
