**$10k MRR path:** Sell 100 seats at $199 in cohorts of 20; recruit 5 mentors on revenue share; run on Zoom/Discord + a simple portal.

**Moat:** Demonstrated outcomes + library of rubrics, checklists, and before/after artifacts; referrals from visible wins.

**Kill test (14 days):** Can you pre-sell 40 seats with a one-pager and 3 mentor profiles?
