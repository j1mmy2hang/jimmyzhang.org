### Floor 1: The Explicit (Conscious Strategy)
*We are aware of these variables. This is the realm of Logic, Law, and War.*

| State | Archetype | Definition | Strategic Nature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Me: Yes / You: Yes** | **1. The Consensus** | Common Knowledge. | **Coordination.** The basis of contracts and reality. |
| **Me: Yes / You: No** | **2. The Edge** | Asymmetric Information. | **Advantage.** Used for leverage, trading, or surprise. |
| **Me: No / You: Yes** | **3. The Blindspot** | Your private knowledge. | **Risk.** Where I am vulnerable to your deception. |
| **Me: No / You: No** | **4. The Frontier** | Known Unknowns. | **Discovery.** Science, R&D, the questions we are asking. |

### Floor 2: The Implicit (Subconscious Reality)
*We possess or lack this knowledge without realizing it. This is the realm of Psychology, Intuition, and Fate.*

| State | Archetype | Definition | Strategic Nature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Me: Yes / You: Yes** | **5. The Culture** | Shared implicit assumptions. | **Cohesion.** "The Vibe." We act in sync without speaking. |
| **Me: Yes / You: No** | **6. The Instinct** | Unknown Knowns. | **Talent.** I can do it, but I can't explain how (Tacit). |
| **Me: No / You: Yes** | **7. The Tell** | You read me better than I do. | **Leakage.** You sense my intent before I formulate it. |
| **Me: No / You: No** | **8. The Void** | Unknown Unknowns. | **Chaos.** Black Swans. The thing that kills us both. |

## First Principles: The Three Questions

For any piece of information between two agents:

1. **POSSESSION**: Who has it? (Me / You / Both / Neither)
2. **AWARENESS**: Who knows they have it? (Conscious / Unconscious)
3. **META-AWARENESS**: Who knows the other's state? (Transparent / Opaque)

---

## The Core Insight: Two Separate Systems

The confusion comes from mixing **individual epistemology** with **relational epistemology**.

### System 1: The Rumsfeld Square (Individual)
**"What do I know about MY knowledge?"**

|                | **I Know I Have It** | **I Don't Know I Have It** |
|----------------|---------------------|---------------------------|
| **I Have It**  | Known Known         | Unknown Known (Instinct)  |
| **I Don't Have It** | Known Unknown  | Unknown Unknown (Void)    |

### System 2: The Johari Window (Relational)
**"Who has it between us?"**

|                | **You Have It** | **You Don't Have It** |
|----------------|----------------|----------------------|
| **I Have It**  | Commons        | My Leverage          |
| **I Don't Have It** | My Blindside | Shared Ignorance    |
