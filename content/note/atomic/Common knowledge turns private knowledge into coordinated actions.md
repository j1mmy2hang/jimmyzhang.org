[[Possessing shared information is insufficient to launch collective action due to the coordination problem.]]

To break the loop, we need a mechanism that instantly converts **Private Knowledge** (which is isolated and paralyzed) into **Common Knowledge** (which is connected and actionable)

**The Public Signal** -- an event that **everyone sees, and everyone sees everyone else seeing.**

**Triangle of Gazes**

- **Private Knowledge:** Two people look at an object <u>through separate keyholes</u>. They both see it, but they don't see each other. (The Loop exists).
- **Common Knowledge:** The walls are removed. I see the object. I see you looking at the object. And I see you looking at me looking at the object.

Example: The Emperor's New Clothes, the kid's shout

> To bridge the gap between **Knowing** and **Doing**, we require a **Public Signal** that generates **Common Knowledge**.
