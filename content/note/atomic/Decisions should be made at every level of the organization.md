❌ Only senior executives make decisions. 
✅ Making good decisions is a crucial skill *at every level.*

> These apparently low-level decisions are extremely important in a knowledge-based organization. Knowledge workers are supposed to know more about their areas of specialization—for example, tax accounting—than anybody else, so their decisions are likely to have an impact throughout the company.
