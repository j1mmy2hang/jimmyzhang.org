The Hallmark of intelligence is not that it always succeeds, 
but the fact that it can fail once twice, thrice, 
but never truly fails,
for it learns each time,
and given time and freedom to grow
it will always succeeds.

智力的机制不是避开失败，而是将失败纳入成长的循环
学习的本质是自我更新
智能的根源在于可塑性和自我重组的能力

一个生长性系统的自毁与重建。
