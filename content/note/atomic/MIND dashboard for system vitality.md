The goal is to move from maximizing "growth" to maximizing **Vitality** using the **MIND Dashboard**. Every nation, city, and company should audit themselves against these four capitals (Capitals multiply; if one is zero, the total is zero).

- **M – Material Capital (Physical Health):**
    - **Measure:** Energy Return on Investment (EROEI), resource circularity, and ecological regeneration.
    - **Goal:** Ensure the physical planet is being restored, not liquidated.
        
- **I – Intelligence Capital (Knowledge):**
    - **Measure:** The creation of new knowledge (not just data), scientific output, and the speed of learning in the population.
    - **Goal:** A population and AI system that gets smarter every day.
        
- **N – Network Capital (Trust):**
    - **Measure:** Social trust levels, transaction friction, and connectivity.
    - **Goal:** High-trust networks where information and value flow without resistance.

[[Economics value is determined by network position]]
    
- **D – Diversity Capital (Resilience):**
    - **Measure:** Economic complexity, biodiversity, and variety of supply chains.
    - **Goal:** Avoid "monocultures" (which are efficient but brittle) to ensure the system can survive shocks.

## Individual Human

> A flourishing human, like a flourishing civilization, does not maximize one of these capitals. They cultivate a balanced portfolio. They tend to their health and their environment(M). They never stop learning(I). They nurture deep relationships(N). And they stay open to new experiences and possibilities(D).
