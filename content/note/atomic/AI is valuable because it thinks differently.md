The real power comes from its inhuman nature.

> “Our most important thinking machines will not be machines that can think what we think faster, better, but those that **think what we can’t think.**”

AI excels at statistical thinking, seeing patterns in massive data sets, and maintaining relentless focus—things humans are notoriously bad at. Kelly calls this **“alien intelligence.”**

**Lesson:** Don't limit your AI strategy to automating human tasks. Look for problems that require a fundamentally different kind of thinking. Use AI to analyze market data, predict supply chain disruptions, or find scientific correlations that no human team ever could.
