e.g. the bicycle / zipper problem

If asked, _“Do you understand how a bicycle works?”_, most people say _yes_ confidently.    
But when asked to **draw** a bicycle that would actually function, a surprising number of people make basic mistakes—putting the chain on the wrong side, connecting both wheels to pedals, missing key frame supports, etc.
