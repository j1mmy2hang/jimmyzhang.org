给 AI 写 prompt
是探索自己真正想要什么，在怎么思考问题的过程。

有些东西在我们脑子里，我们自己都不一定清楚我们自己想要什么。
如果你想要 AI 给你好的答案，你必须先用语言梳理清楚自己到底想要什么。

> One benefit of a chat interface for AI is that in order to steer it toward a particular outcome, we learn to be good at articulating exactly those likes and dislikes. A good start here might be to recognize that you do like or dislike something, that it evokes a certain feeling in you without necessarily being able to explain that feeling. Over time, you learn to recognize that feeling and find the words to describe that feeling.
