### The rise of AI & the need to re-unite

The era of nuclear deterrence that produced the "long peace" is ending, and a new era of deterrence based on software and AI is beginning; yet the West, particularly the U.S. defense establishment, is dangerously behind, still focused on legacy hardware while its engineering talent is misdirected toward trivial consumer culture.

The West is suffering from a dangerous "winner's fallacy," a complacent belief that its liberal democratic ideals guarantee victory, causing its technological elite to hesitate in developing the hard power of AI weaponry that is essential for deterrence and survival in the 21st century.

> “One age of deterrence, the atomic age, is ending, and a new era of deterrence built on AI is set to begin... The twenty-first century is the software century. And the fate of the United States, and its allies, depends on the ability of their defense and intelligence agencies to evolve, and briskly.”
