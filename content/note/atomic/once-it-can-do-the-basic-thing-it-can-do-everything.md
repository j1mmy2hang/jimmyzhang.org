---
title: "Once it can do the basic thing, it can do everything"

created: 2025-05-05
---
1. **Bootstrapping**: Mastery of a core skill enables the system to learn and build upon itself.
2. **Skill Composability**: Complex tasks are combinations of simpler ones—doing the basics allows assembling higher-level abilities.
3. **Recursive Improvement**: Systems that can self-improve can rapidly evolve from simple to highly advanced capabilities.
4. **Historical Pattern**: Human progress often begins with mastering a simple foundation that unlocks exponential development.
5. **Applies to General Systems**: This logic holds best for adaptable, learning systems—not for narrow or rigid ones.
