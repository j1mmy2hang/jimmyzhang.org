Trap: Shifting the Burden to the Intervenor — Addiction
Source: a destructive reinforcing loop set in motion by an intervention designed to correct the problem undermines the self-maintaining capacity of the original system
The Way out:
- Avoid getting in one -- beware of symptom-relieving or signal-denying policies or practices that don’t really address the problem.
- Restructuring the system -- to strengthen the ability of the system to shoulder its own burdens
