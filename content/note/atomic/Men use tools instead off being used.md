## Most knowledge / service jobs

tasks are not fixed or predetermined
workers need to define and decide their tasks themselves
tools / machines are only means to their ends
--> Workers *use* tools

## Most manufacturing / transportation jobs

tasks are predetermined and fixed
the job is machine-paced -- the work pace is set by machines (or tools), and workers’ role is primarily to serve the machines

Machines need human to operate them
These jobs are the job that are meant to be entirely automized in the future

--> Humans are *being used by* the machines
