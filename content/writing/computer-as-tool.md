---
title: Computer as tool, not space for thinking
published: 2026-04-10
---
I recently rediscovered the analog notebook as a powerful tool for thinking. 

Instead of typing in Obsidian as usual, I would now spent about half an hour doodling and writing on my notebook every day before bedtime to help me make sense of a lot of my thinking. 

I realized that notebook has always been my favorite space for thinking before I have my first laptop, and I used to be so passionate about note-taking on paper. It also made me realize that laptop should become more of a tool instead of space for thinking — I should open my laptop for a specific task to get done, not just using it as the default space to spent time in. 

We, at the end of the day, still live in the physical world.
