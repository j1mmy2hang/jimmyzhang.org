## 0. What is telos

Telos is the why before all whys -- the irreducible purpose that underlies all my thinking and action. 

My Telos consists of two statement -- of myself and of the world. 

## 1. Self Statement

> I hope to deeply connect with myself and my environment achieve a state of agentic being. 

**Connection** -- Meaning arises from connection. 

- Myself -- my physical and spiritual body, how i think and work
- Natural environment -- how my actions and living is connected with Earth
- Social environment (people) -- how my existence impacts and is impacted by my relationship to other people

**Agentic Being** -- the state of being a fully conscious, self-directed creator of one's existence.

1. To be aware of and able to exercise intentional control over my emotions, body, thoughts, and actions — and their impact on my environment. 
2. To channel the awareness and control to live my life to the fullest -- to craft my own experience, and to become a creator not consumer of my life. 

## 2. World Statement

> I hope to empower universal human agency. 

Through

- **Individual support** -- to empower individuals to find and realize their humanistic value through learning
- **Structural rework** -- rework the modern system of money & work to prepare for the transition towards a post-scarcity post-organizational world
