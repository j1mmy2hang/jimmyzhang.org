## Common fallacies

The Zero-Sum Fallacy -- [[Government policies in the interest of helping one side in transactions will lead to both sides being worse off]]

The Fallacy of Composition -- [[What's good for a part is not necessarily good for all]]

The Post Hoc Fallacy -- [[Subsequent events does not indicate causation]]

The Chess-Pieces Fallacy -- [[People are complex creatures that cannot be manipulated]]

The Open-Ended Fallacy -- [[More of something good is not always better for the whole]]
