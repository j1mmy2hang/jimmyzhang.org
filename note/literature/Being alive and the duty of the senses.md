The morality of life and the importance of experieces

> I am an agnostic. I don't believe in life after death in a way that would make sense to us as living people. I think this is it. In this time, we have our bodies, we have eyes, we have tongues, arms, and legs. It is our purpose, our duty, to use all of those things to the fullest extent while we have them.

> What exists in the realm of flavors and textures that is worth exploring? Why am I so crazy? Why am I pursuing these things? It's just: **I have a tongue. So let's see what we can do with that.**"
