[[工作体现人的价值]]

> [!summary]
> A constant fear of death is a good source of motivation. if harnesesed well. 

What if I died tomorrow? What if today's my last day? 
If I died like this, what are my biggest regrets?

Embracing the inevitability of death can help executives build *meaning* into their work and leave the *legacies* they desire.

One should be more afraid of a meaningless existence than death itself.
> Do not fear death so much, but rather the inadequate life. -- Bertolt Brecht

- [Thoughts of death make us yearn for power.](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/insights/why-thoughts-death-make-us-yearn-power)
	- The prospect of death motivates us to protect ourselves by bolstering our feelings of self-worth. 
	- Feeling powerful is one way to increase an individual’s feelings of self-esteem
