## Fundamental Difference

Art = to make great new things

- great — define greatness / what's beautiful yourself 
- new — not done by others, no template

School project

- have to conform to the *rubric* to get good marks; standard of good being created for you by others
- mass-produced; 流水线作业，无数个人之前做过，无数个人之后还要做；固定的标准格式 -- 好的/合格的作品都千篇一律

You can only do well on a school assignment. You can only achieve greatness when you are creating art. 

---

## 两者的关系 -- 本质上冲突，实际上可融合

对于有些只忠实于自己的心灵忠实于艺术的人来说，被迫做学校作业/生产流水线产品要承受极大的痛苦。

当然也可以把学校任务做的和艺术一样，用 artistic craftsmanship 去对待 -- 这样做出来的分基本不会差。但是只要是 school project 就有 requirement 就要去牺牲一部分 artistic agency，只能做到带着脚链跳舞，尽可能放大 art 的部分。

擅长这样做的人并不少 -- 他们通常体制内外都是胜者。体制内他们能在可能的范围内掌握规则利用规则，甚至打破规则，同时获得“高分”；体制外他们会发挥自己真正的 artistic talent to make great new things

e.g Bill Gates Delaware assignment

---

## Bottom line = Art comes first

but still -- make art if possible. 
Arts are marked by their freedom.
Arts are arts not because they are good (according to anyone's standards), but because they (re)define what is good themselves.
