[[The logic of violence is the ultimate determinant of social structures]]

For the past 500 years, technology consistently increased the returns to large-scale violence, leading to ever-larger and more powerful states. 

The 500-year trend of centralizing power has reversed. The driver of this reversal is microtechnology. 

*   **It disarms the state:** By making assets digital, mobile, and concealable through encryption, it removes them from the coercive grasp of government. The state's ability to tax and regulate is evaporating.
*   **It empowers the individual:** For the first time, an individual can possess assets that are truly private and secure, protected by mathematics rather than by the state's armed forces. The individual can now operate on a global stage, independent of the geographic constraints that once made them a captive subject.
