Mankind is indeed capable of doing an amazingly wide diversity of things. 
Humanity is a multipurpose tool. 

But the way to apply productively mankind's great range is to bring to bear a large number of individual capabilities on one task. 
It is concentration in which all faculties are focused on one achievement.
