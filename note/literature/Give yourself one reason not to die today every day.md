When we are trying to ask ourselves what's the meaning of life, on the other side we are essentially asking why don't we die or commit suicide today. 

Giving yourself one reason not to die today, small as it may be, is essential in helping yourself find your goal, love, passion, and gradually the meaning of life. 

Your meaning of life doesn't have to be something big.
