## The Gap between "Shared Belief" and "Coordination"

Possessing shared information is **necessary but insufficient**.  
To move from "believing" to "doing," we need to cross a specific threshold. We need to solve what Game Theory calls the **Coordination Problem**.

Coordination problem = when **we both want the same thing**, but we can only get it if we act in the **exact same way at the exact same time**.

Infinite hall of mirrors: I am not just choosing what I want. I am choosing **what I think you think I think you are going to choose.** 

> It is not enough for everyone to know the truth. We are paralyzed until we are certain that others know we know the truth.
