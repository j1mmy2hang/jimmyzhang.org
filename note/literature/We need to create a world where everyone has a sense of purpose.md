Finding your purpose isn't enough. You also have to create that sense of purpose for others. 

[[Purpose creates true happiness]]
[[The real impact makers are the ones that are able to impact others]]

> Every generation has its defining works. More than 300,000 people work to put a man on the moon, including that janitor. Millions of volunteers immunize children around the world against polio. Millions of more people built the Hoover Dam and other great projects. These projects didn't just provide purpose for the people doing those jobs. It gave our whole country a sense of pride that we could do great things.

Does it have to be a common purpose tho.... can people all have their individual purpose...

## How

- By taking on big projects together
	- Let's do big things, not just to create progress, but to create purpose. ([[Effort creates meaning]])
	- [[Don't wait for everything to be ready to begin]]
	- [[Don't be afraid of making mistakes and having problems]]
	- [[Being idealistic requires being prepared to be misunderstood]]
- By redefining equality so that everyone has the freedom to pursue their purpose
	- [[Great successses come from having the freedom to fail]]
	- [[Measure the development of a society by the number of people who have a role they find meaningful]]
	- [[A society needs to give everyone the freedom to pursue purpose]]
- By building communities around the world
	- [[All changes starts local]]
	- [[我们时代的冲突是开放和逆开放力量的对抗]]
