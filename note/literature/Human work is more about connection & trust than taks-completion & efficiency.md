True human work is more about task completion -- which AI can do better

Trust, the bedrock of successful organizations, is forged through in-person interaction.

A truly human future of work is not a binary choice between home and office, but a re-evaluation of work itself, moving toward a "craft" model that prioritizes human skills, trust, and meaningful relationships over the dehumanizing pursuit of machine-like efficiency.

Knowledge work thrives on the "unproductive" moments—the chitchat, the coffee breaks, the shared experiences—that build the connective tissue of an organization
