1. Humans take actions based on their knowledge / model / understanding of the world
2. Human receive feedback from the reality (in the form of information) which help them better their model & correct their assumptions / force them to create new models

We can never gain direct access to the reality; but only through our model (of [[Language shapes worldview and perception|language]]) and the feedback from the reality

[[All knowledge about the world is a model]]
