## Definition

Defining a problem means

1. what you want to achieve
2. what's in the way of getting there

## Problem

People too often end up solving the wrong problem
[[many-projects-fail-because-they-attempt-to-solve-non-existent-problems]]

The first lens into a problem really reveals what the real problem is. 

**We've been taught our whole lives to *solve* problems.** -- But when it comes to defining problems, we have less experience. Things are often uncertain. We seldom have all the information.

## Importance

The way we define a problem shapes everyone's perspective about it and 
determines the solutions. 

> To understand is to know what to do. 
> -- Ludwig Wittgenstein

---

> SAFEGUARD: Build a problem-solution firewall. Separate the problem-defining phase of the decision-making process from the problem-solving phase.
