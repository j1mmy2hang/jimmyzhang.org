> **Doing what we already know how to do takes the world from 1 to n, adding more of something familiar. But every time we create something new, we go from 0 to 1.**

> Our task today is to find singular ways to create the new things that will make the future not just different, but better—to go from 0 to 1.

> Spreading old ways to create wealth around the worldwill result in devastation, not riches. In a world of scarce resources, globalization without new technology is unsustainable."
