Will the LLM labs capture all applications or are there green pastures for LLM apps? ([[vertical-ai-that-only-wraps-base-model-capabilities-has-no-future]])

> "LLM labs will trend to graduate the generally capable college student, but LLM apps will organize, finetune and actually animate teams of them into deployed professionals in specific verticals by supplying private data, sensors and actuators and feedback loops."

- **LLM companies** (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) → provide general-purpose AI capabilities
- **"Cursor for X" apps** → take those capabilities and specialize them for specific industries/use cases with domain-specific data, tools, and workflows
