In order to get the results we desire, we must
1. create the space to reason in our thoughts, feelings, and actions
2. deliberately use that space to think clearly

## Creating Space

The first step in improving our outcomes is to train ourselves to identify the moments when judgment is called for in the first place, and pause to create space to think clearly.

Strength is the power to press pause on your defaults and exercise good judgment.

The person who can take a step back for a second, center themselves, and get out of the moment will outperform the person who can’t.
