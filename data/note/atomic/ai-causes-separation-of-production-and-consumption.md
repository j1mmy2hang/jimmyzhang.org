AI 只生产，不消费
金钱在 AI infinite supply 面前失效

Traditional capitalism relies on a heartbeat: **Produce, Earn, Consume.**  
Humans build the cars, earn the wages, and buy the cars. The cycle sustains itself.

AI stops the heart. It is a tireless producer with zero appetite.

- **Infinite Supply:** AI codes, designs, and manufactures at near-zero marginal cost.
- **Zero Demand:** AI buys no groceries, pays no rent, and books no flights.
