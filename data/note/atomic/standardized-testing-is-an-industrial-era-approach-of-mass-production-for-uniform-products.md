> Standardized testing for student, school, school system, and curriculum evaluation is an industrial era approach of mass production for uniform products. The complex society of the information age needs diverse skills and, needless to say, individual desires and talents will not be fulfilled through mass production of students with standardized, and ultimately lowest common capabilities.
