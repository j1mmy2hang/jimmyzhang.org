---
title: "珍惜这次去荷兰 start anew 的机会"

tags:
  - opportunity
  - cherish
---
Cherish your chance of starting anew, just bringing your necessities and the things and only the things that matter to you with value. 

You won't have these kinds of chances a lot later in your life, especially as your material wealth and possessions grow.

You will only become burdened with more and more stuff that stops you from just running away & starting a new life. 

> [!important]
> Cherish the chance -- grasp it & utilize it to construct a better version of your life and yourself.  
> This is the true starting point, the zero point.
