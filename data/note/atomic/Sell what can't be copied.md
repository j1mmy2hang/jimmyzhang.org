Flowing forces a radical re-evaluation of what is valuable. 

When copies are free, you need to sell things that cannot be copied.

- Immediacy: Offer early access, beta versions, or instant delivery.
- Personalization: Tailor the service to each individual user.
- Interpretation: Sell support, training, and expert guidance.
- Authenticity: Provide certified, reliable, and secure versions.
- Accessibility: Manage content in the cloud for seamless, multi-device access.
- Embodiment: Create valuable live experiences (concerts, conferences, live support).
- Patronage: Build tools that allow fans to support creators directly.
- Discoverability: Become the trusted filter that helps customers find the best content.
