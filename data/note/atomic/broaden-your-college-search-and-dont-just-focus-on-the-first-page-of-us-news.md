Don't let [[brandnames-and-selectivity-dont-matter-that-much|brand names & selectivity]] limit your choice

Remember, many students start their college search in the fall with aspirational dreams that end in the spring with a cold reality: you might only get into your “likely schools,” so be sure they’re colleges you’d want to attend and not just placeholders on a list.

Savvy students willing to look beyond the brand-name sellers can find great schools that are buyers.
