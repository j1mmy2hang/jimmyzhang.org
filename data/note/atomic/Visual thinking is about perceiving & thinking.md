The difference between verbal and visual thinking is not just the difference between reading words and looking at pictures.

> Visual thinking is not about seeing, per se. Everyone sees unless they are blind. Visual thinking refers to the way the mind works, to the way we perceive.

> Visual thinking is not about how we *see* but about how the brain processes information; how we *perceive* and *think*.
