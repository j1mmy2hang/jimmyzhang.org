title: "Economics seeks to open the blackbox of the world, create models, and optimize indicators"

created: 2025-09-26
---
Economics became the "science of thinking in terms of models -- building a highly simplified model based on strong assumptions, proving a conclusion within that model, and then acting as if that conclusion is a proven fact about the messy real world.

Economics chose to open the black box and, finding it empty, filled it with a simplistic fantasy of a profit-maximizing firm. Cybernetics, in contrast, respected the system's complexity and focused on concepts like feedback and "homeostasis" (the tendency of a system to return to a stable state, whether optimal or not).

-- [[the-unaccountability-machine]]

This winning philosophy redesigned our institutions into rigid machines obsessed with maximizing a single, simple goal (like shareholder value). To achieve this, it forced them to ignore complex realities and systematically cut feedback loops, creating "[[the-crisis-of-accountability-sinks-in-modern-institutions-no-one-is-responsible|accountability sinks]]" to shield decision-makers from the consequences of their actions.
