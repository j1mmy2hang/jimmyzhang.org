It's never too early to begin. 
Don't wait for everything to be ready becayse it never will. 
But that shouldn't stop you from starting. 
--> <u>Work with the garage door open</u> & grow through the process

> No one knows how when they begin. Ideas don't come out fully formed. They only become clear as you work on them. You just have to get started. Movies and pop culture get this all wrong. The idea of a single eureka moment is a dangerous lie. It makes us feel inadequate since we haven't had hours. It prevents people with seeds of good ideas from getting started.
