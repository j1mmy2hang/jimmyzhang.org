## Pinned Posts

The one and only principle of this server -- Learning in Public

- Traditional Learning -- every one learning the same curriculum, in competition with each other
- Learning in Public -- every one following their own passion and interests, and learn collective in community by sharing with each other and interacting with other's learnings

Learning in public is fundamentally about 

- Sharing your learning and failure -- This is a safe space. 
- Interacting with other people's learning -- Ask questions, pose alternative perspectives, or simply like their post to show that "I see you".

The full philosophy can be found here -- https://www.swyx.io/learn-in-public and here https://nesslabs.com/learning-in-public

I have always wanted to build a social media / space / platform optimized for the sharing of learning and deep thinking. 

People argue that there are a lot of things wrong with our mainstream social media (instagram, wechat moments, X, etc.). There indeed are.
