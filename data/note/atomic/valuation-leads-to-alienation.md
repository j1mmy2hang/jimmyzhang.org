To obtain value, a thing must be alienable

> "We who do the valuation must be able to stand apart from the thing we are pricing. We have to be able to conceive of separating ourselves from it. I may be fond of my wristwatch but I can put a value on it because I can imagine parting with it. But my heart has no market value..."  

**这就是大公司让你感到不适的根本原因：**  
商业的底层逻辑是追求 Value。为了计算你的劳动能换多少钱，公司必须把你的人格、你的情感、你的生命时间，从你身上“剥离”出来，变成电子表格上的数字、KPI 和工时。[[资本主义组织社会 mass-production 对人的异化]]
