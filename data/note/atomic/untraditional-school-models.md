## Montessori

Founder -- [Dr. Maria Montessori](https://montessori-ami.org/resource-library/facts/biography-maria-montessori)
[The Montessori Method](https://www.bol.com/nl/nl/f/the-montessori-method/9200000024825023/)

> Montessori is an education and a perspective that moves from *learning* to *application* through <u>curiosity and creativity</u>. -- [Association Montessori Internationale](https://montessori-ami.org/about-ami)

> Children learn at their own pace, exploring advanced academic concepts and skills through *hands-on materials* and *freedom of choice*. -- [Hershey Montessori School](https://hershey-montessori.org/)


## Waldorf

Founder -- Rudolf Steiner
holistic approach

> Physical education, handicrafts, the arts and respect for the natural world and each other are of equal importance to proficiency in mathematics and literacy. -- [International Waldorf School The Hague](https://www.internationalwaldorfschool.nl/)

> Waldorf pedagogy is based on the idea that education should address the whole child—their physical well-being, their social and emotional well-being, and their intellectual, cognitive, and spiritual development. -- [Associaiton of Wardolf Schools of North America](https://www.waldorfeducation.org/what-is-waldorf-education/)
