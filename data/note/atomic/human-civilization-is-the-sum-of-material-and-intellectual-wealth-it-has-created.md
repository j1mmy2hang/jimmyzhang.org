- Material wealth -- The Pyramids, the Great Wall, WTC, etc.
	- tangible
	- prone to decay
- Intellectual wealth -- Relativity, Literature, etc.
	- intangible
		- need to rely on a medium
	- not prone to decay by itself
		- but again relies on a physical form
