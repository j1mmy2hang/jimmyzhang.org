## Problem Statement

- Micro -- Most student-led projects fail
- Macro -- UWC see no output / result in its educational movement

Define "fail"

- never got out of paper in the first place
- got into execution, but
	- imaginary / limited impact
	- unsustainable & short-lived

Examples

- CSC Impact Week
- UWCM YSE

## Possible Reason

- Drive -- lack of true passion / doing project for the sake of doing (school requirement, college application)
- Plan / Thinking -- plan doesn't make sense at all (trying to solve an imaginary problem, no knowledge, etc.)
- Execution / Management -- lack of thinking framework / PM system

Other reason

- UWC campuses isolated from each other --> missed opportunities for meaningful collab /  scaling

## Sginificance

- UWC with its mission in solving world problems need successful student projects with lasting impacts to strive toward its mission -- UWC students could have made much larger impacts with its funding & resources
	- currently funding & resources go in, but nothing (output / result) comes out

## Solution Offered

- Facilitation
	- connecting students with expertise in PM / thinking framworks / leaderhsip (Minerva)
	- facilitating student projects in finding passion, project design, & PM
- Communication
	- [[UWC TV, Network & System Buildup Project]]
	- A Cross-campus platform for the communication & advertisement for student projects
- Measurement & Tracking
	- impact tracker / measurer / dashboard
	- An action-focused mission control center
	- Key Question -- How do we measure the effectiveness of a school and of an education?
