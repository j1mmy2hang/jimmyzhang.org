Contemporary American society outsources its most morally compromising yet essential tasks—from guarding prisoners and killing by drone to slaughtering animals and extracting fossil fuels—to a hidden, disadvantaged class of workers, allowing the privileged to maintain a clean conscience and creating a profound moral inequality.

## Characteristics of Dirty Work

- It causes substantial harm (to people, animals, or the environment).
- It is seen as morally compromised by "good people" in society.
- It is psychologically and emotionally damaging to the people who perform it.
- Most importantly, it relies on a **tacit mandate** from the rest of us. We benefit from it, we want it done, but we don't want to know the messy details, so we delegate it and then disavow responsibility.
