"Transformative Education"
--> who transforming whom?
--> from what to what?

## Hidden assumptions

- there is a better state (current state --> desire state)
- students are shapable
- students need to be transformed -- a deficit view on learners; consent for being transformed?
- only external forces can transform students -- the power of self-transformation?
- the educator knows what transformation is needed
- education is justified to be used as a tool for transforming others -- power dynamics between "the transformer" and "the transformed"
